## Creating an Account

### Step 1: Register

1. Visit the homepage
2. Click **"Sign Up"** in the top right
3. Fill in your details:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Phone Number
   - Password (minimum 6 characters)
   - Location (allow location access or enter manually)

4. Click **"Create Account"**

### Step 2: Verify Your Account

1. Check your email for verification link
2. Click the link to verify your account
3. You're ready to book services!

### Step 3: Complete Your Profile

1. Go to **Profile** ‚Üí **Edit Profile**
2. Add your complete address
3. Add a profile picture (optional)
4. Save changes

## Booking a Service

### Step 1: Browse Services

1. Click **"Book a Service"** on the homepage
2. Browse available services
3. Click on the service you need

### Step 2: Provide Details

1. **Service Type**: Selected automatically
2. **Location**: Confirm or update your location
3. **Schedule**: Choose immediate or schedule for later
4. **Additional Notes**: Add any special instructions

### Step 3: Confirm Booking

1. Review booking details
2. Click **"Confirm Booking"**
3. Wait for helper assignment (usually < 30 seconds)

### Step 4: Helper Assignment

Once a helper is assigned, you'll receive:
- **SMS notification** with helper details
- **In-app notification** with helper info
- **Email confirmation** with booking details

**Helper Information Includes:**
- Name and photo
- Rating and reviews
- Estimated arrival time
- Contact number

## FAQs

**Q: How quickly will a helper be assigned?**  
A: Usually within 30 seconds. Maximum 15 minutes.

**Q: Can I choose a specific helper?**  
A: Currently, helpers are auto-assigned based on proximity and availability. Favorite helper feature coming soon!

**Q: What if the helper doesn't arrive?**  
A: Contact support immediately. We'll assign a new helper or provide a full refund.

**Q: Can I schedule a service for later?**  
A: Yes! Choose "Schedule for Later" when booking.

**Q: What if I'm not satisfied with the service?**  
A: Report the issue within 24 hours. We'll investigate and provide a resolution.

**Q: Are helpers verified?**  
A: Yes! All helpers undergo background checks and skill verification.

**Q: Can I tip the helper?**  
A: Yes! You can add a tip during payment.

**Q: What if I need to reschedule?**  
A: Cancel the current booking and create a new one. No fee if cancelled before assignment.
